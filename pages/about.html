About this Event

ECO-INNOVATION FOR ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY 2018.

ECO-AFRICA Conference (EFC) is one of leading African forums on the green economy. It brings together world -class experts around Africa to directly focus on advancing the global green economy and sustainability agenda, achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals and implementing the recommendations of COP 21 & 22.

ECO-AFRICA is the premier international platform to drive dialogue and action among industry stakeholders, and build partnerships and collaboration by connecting creative minds across different sectors. The summit will showcases innovative solutions and leading practices to inspire change, and aims to align stakeholders with policy makers to create a facilitative environment and ecosystem for sustainable green growth that address current and future needs.

It is a critical need to address now, Air pollution in Nigeria kills more than 6 million people every year, is the biggest environmental health risk of our time. Airborne pollutants are responsible for about one third of deaths from stroke, chronic respiratory disease, and lung cancer, as well as one quarter of deaths from heart attack. Air pollution is also fundamentally altering our climate, with profound impacts on the health of the planet.

Transport activity is increasing around Nigerian cities as economies grow, which means that the sector’s emissions are also on the rise due to high demand of over population. That’s largely because 95 per cent of the transportation system energy still comes from fossil fuels.

Nigerian seas and oceans are facing unprecedented threats as a result of human activity more especially in Niger Delta Region. Every year, an estimated of million tonnes of chemicals and plastic waste end up in Nigerian oceans. At the same time, climate change is damaging coral reefs and other key ecosystems; leaving the water untreated.
